Hello, my name is Joseph, and this is the game 4 PONG! You can play this game solo, but I believe it's always more fun to play games with friends. In this interactive game, up to four people can join in the action, each with their own paddle! Or, if you prefer, you can simply play one-on-one with a friend:)

  • top paddle = left/right keys
  • right paddle = up/down keys
  • bottom paddle = v/b keys
  • left paddle = w/s keys
